THE TRUTH REVOLUTION

THE ERA OF BLIND TRUST IS OVER.

What the label claims and what’s actually inside should never be two different stories. Truth Sachet puts verification in the consumer’s hands through one universal experience built around many small, targeted reactions.

ONE FORMAT · MANY SIGNALS

A MICRO-LAB
YOU CAN SEE.

The proposed sachet combines controlled sample preparation, a calibration field, batch-aware reagent mapping, and a grid of independent test zones.

01

The transparent sachet

Drop, crush, squeeze and watch. A contained workflow designed to remove guesswork.

02

Dual extraction

Separate sealed liquid pods can expose water- and alcohol-responsive compounds.

03

20–40 micro-zones

Each zone is assigned to a supported ingredient, filler, marker or contaminant.

04

Batch-aware calibration

A printed colour grid and QR mapping help normalize the image and identify the test lot.
