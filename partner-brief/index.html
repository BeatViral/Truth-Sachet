PRODUCT OVERVIEW

Product Overview

Truth Sachet is a consumer at-home screening kit concept for powdered supplements and food powders. The goal is to help consumers screen for authenticity and adulteration signals before relying on a product.

Important note This is not a medical diagnostic product. It is intended as a consumer screening tool. Lab confirmation may be recommended where needed.

/ 004

CONSERVATIVE SCREENING TARGETS

Target Screening Signals

Protein Powders

  • Broad protein presence / protein-strength signal
  • Starch, flour, carbohydrate, or sugar dilution
  • Suspicious mismatch against label claims
  • Possible amino spiking / nitrogen manipulation if feasible

Sports Supplements

  • Caffeine presence or rough strength indicator
  • Possible stimulant/adulterant indicators where feasible
  • Banned-substance risk indicators where feasible, or lab-confirmation pathway if not suitable for at-home screening

Herbal / Ayurvedic / Spice Powders

  • Starch/flour dilution
  • Synthetic dye or artificial colour adulteration
  • Weak or missing marker signal for claimed herb/spice where feasible
  • Other common powder adulteration indicators

General Supplement Authenticity

  • Whether the sample broadly matches expected chemical behaviour for the claimed product type
  • Whether the reaction suggests dilution, substitution, or suspicious formulation
  • Whether lab confirmation should be recommended

- caffeine presence or rough strength
